Business
Guide

Probot's business model entails a subscription-based service, offering access to its website and chatbot functionalities for a monthly fee of 19 INR per user. This model ensures sustainable revenue generation while providing affordable access to mental health support, business assistance, and student career guidance services. Additionally, Probot may explore additional revenue streams through partnerships, premium features, or targeted advertising in the future to enhance its monetization strategy.
